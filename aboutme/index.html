All Photos About Me

John Ray

Hello there! I am a Theoretical Physics student at Lancaster University, and a keen Photographer and Astronomer. I made this website to have an easy place to show my photos, and to also get extra practice at coding with HTML / JavaScript. You can access all my photos on the "All Photos" option, which links to a part of the website that Orlando.PB made and hosts (check out his more professional site here!). Alternatively, I've put some of my current favourite photos on the other pages of the website. I'll try to update this website whenever I get the chance (it's still a work in progress at the moment), thanks for reading :)

To contact me, feel free to send an email to me@johnray.co.uk or you can visit my Instagram at john.59.ray.

Useful Links

Astro links:

  • Stellarium (Web Version) - great site for star charts.
  • Clear Outside (link goes to Lancaster location) - good site for forecasts.
  • Siril Manual Processing Tutorial - handy guide to Siril Processing.
  • AuroraWatch (Web Version) - great site for Aurora forecasts.
  • LUAstro.space - LUAstro Society's website (coded and built by me!)
  • LiverpoolAS.org - Liverpool Astronomical Society's website

Bird Photography links:

  • Merlin - excellent website for identifying birds. I recommend the app.
  • Ebird (link goes to Lancashire alerts) - good free alternative to BirdGuides or RBA.
  • MPB - great site for 2nd-hand camera equipment.
