Hiking Photography

Here are a couple of my photos from various hikes, I'll hopefully be able to include GPX data and maps on them in the future, alongside more images and information on each hike. Due to my location, most of these hikes are in England's Lake District, but there's the occasional one from the Yorkshire Dales or Wales. As with most of this website, this page is a work in progress! It will hopefully be updated fully sometime soon.

My first proper hike - an 8hr hike from Lancaster University to Clougha Pike, at the edge of Bowland Forest.
My first hike with LUHC, I climbed Jack's Rake - a Grade I scramble.
Patterdale, a small village in Cumbria.
An image from a weekend trip with LUHC to Snowdonia (Eryri). Unfortunately, there was a rather bad storm at the time, but the group I was with ignored this and hiked up Snowdon anyway.
An image from near the summit of Ingleborough, one of the Yorkshire Three Peaks. The weather wasn't ideal!
An image of Rydal Water from Grasmere. Probably my favourite hike so far.
A bridge over Lancaster Canal on the way to Glasson Dock.
Trees from a Day's Hike around Grizedale Forest, a forest between Coniston and Windermere.
